1. Pre-registration is required even if you are a day attendee.
2. Lodging Information:
* Semi-Private Lodges: Consists of two to five beds per room. Linens are provided, and restrooms are attached or down the hall.
* Economy Cabins/Dorms: These dormitory style cabins are equipped with solid wood frame bunk beds and are carpeted and heated. Bring your own bedding (sheets, sleeping bags, pillows, towels). The cabins share a central bath house.
* RV/Camping: Campers use the central bathhouse.
* Day Use: For people who will attend the retreat for 1, or 2 days with no overnight staying onsite.

5. Scholarship (Need-Based Financial Assistance) Fund:
We do not want anyone to "miss" the retreat because of financial constraints. Participants may apply for scholarships or need-based financial assistance. Scholarships will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis, and we might have to wait till we receive all scholarship requests before deciding. Please get in touch with registration for scholarships.
